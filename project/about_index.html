About Isabelle

Hi! My name is Isabelle, and I am currently a student at Northwestern University. I'm a journalism major with minors in entrepreneurship and Integrated Marketing and Communications. It's obviously a complicated and strange mix of studies, but I feel that it perfectly describes me. I am both a writer and a problem-solver. Ideally, I'd like to work in a space that embraces this duality with a focus on innovation. In my personal and professional life, I'm very focused on growth and problem-solving: finding pain points and building a solution. Solutions require different perspectives, modes of thinking, and academic backgrounds. So, I'm focused on collecting knowledge that will make me the best problem solver I can be and allow me to foster innovation that improves the world around me.
